ABOUT



Metacognition 2020, the annual technical fiesta of Galgotias College of Engineering and technology. This colossal fiesta is indeed a treat for all the tech enthusiasts nationwide to brandish their skills and promulgate their tech abilities.
Metacognition, as the name envisages is a group of ignited cognitive minds and imaginative ideas encompassed at broadening the intellective of students and providing motion to their innovative ideas and research-oriented calibre. It is a celebration of tech-savvy, along with a panel of blue-chip experts and guardians to embark the paths of meritorious techies and encourage them to unleash their innovative skills and win the multidimensional skies of tech reservatories ranging from robotics to LAN gaming to artificial intelligence and intelligent systems. As we celebrate the grandeur of this mega event, this time we introduce the theme of Marvel Cinemeatics to give it a larger than life manifestation. So, gear up to set everything marvellous!!
